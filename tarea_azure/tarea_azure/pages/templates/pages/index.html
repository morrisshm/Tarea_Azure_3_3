Description

The game consists of a simulation of different percussion musical instruments, in which the player will have interaction with different instruments. Each instrument will have its specifications and difficulty of use.

Get Started

Objective

To learn the different percussion instruments and their complexity, as well as to learn and teach how each one sounds.

  • Simulate a gameplay of musical instruments
  • The freedom to learn from the different percussion instruments.
  • The player will have to press the corresponding key using the buttons on the keyboard and start simulating.
  • At the beginning you start with a number of buttons that will be periodically increased.

History and Characters

A student, who wishes to enter a renowned music school. But, for this he needs to learn multiple instruments.
He starts from instruments with little complexity, must take a final exam, which will allow him to see if he has the skills and according to that he will get his place in the music university.

Game mechanics

Phases of the game

Level 1 Easy level
  • Tambourine - Interaction with this instrument will only be one key (S) or (↓).
  • Tarola - Interaction with this instrument will only be one key (S) or (↓).
  • Cymbals - Interaction with this instrument will only be one key (S) or (↓).
Level 2 Medium
  • Bongos - Interaction with this instrument will be 2 keys (A,S) or (←,↓).
  • Xylophone - The interaction with this instrument will be 4 keys (A,S,D,W) or (←,↓,→,↑).
  • Drum - Interaction with this instrument will be 3-key (A,S,D) or (←,↓,→).
Level 3 Hard
  • Drums - Interaction with this instrument will be 5 keys(W,A,S,S,D,T) or (←,↓,→,↑,T).
  • Marimba - Interaction with this instrument will be 6 keys(W,A,S,S,D,T,Y) or (←,↓,→,↑,T).
